Government Schemes for Farmers

This page provides information about various government schemes designed to support farmers. These schemes aim to help farmers with crop production, machinery, fertilizers, and other agricultural needs. The government is committed to empowering farmers through financial assistance, training, and resources to improve their livelihoods and agricultural productivity.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

Provides financial assistance of ‚Çπ6,000 per year to small and marginal farmers.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Crop insurance scheme to protect farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Provides farmers with timely access to credit for agricultural and allied activities.

National Mission for Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

Promotes sustainable agriculture practices to increase productivity and resilience.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Encourages organic farming practices among farmers.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides farmers with soil health cards to improve soil fertility and productivity.

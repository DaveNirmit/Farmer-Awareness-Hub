Water Management for Farmers

Water is one of the most critical resources for agriculture. Proper water management ensures sustainable farming practices, improves crop yields, and conserves this precious resource. However, water scarcity and pollution are growing concerns, making it essential for farmers to adopt efficient water management techniques.

Clean water is vital for irrigation, livestock, and overall farm operations. The scarcity of water due to climate change, overuse, and pollution poses a significant challenge to farmers worldwide. This page highlights the importance of water management and how the Farmer Awareness Hub can help farmers connect with NGOs and government programs to address water-related issues.

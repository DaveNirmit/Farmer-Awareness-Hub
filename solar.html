Solar Energy for Farmers

How Solar Panels Work

Solar panels, also known as photovoltaic (PV) panels, convert sunlight into electricity. They absorb sunlight using photovoltaic cells, generate direct current (DC) electricity, and use an inverter to convert it into alternating current (AC) for use on your farm.
